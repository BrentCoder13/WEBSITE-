About Us

Papz Baliciado Auto Care Center began as a small, mobile service personally handled by its CEO, Papz Baliciado. In the early years, he would travel from one place to another to provide automotive services, sometimes with only a few people to assist him. Most of the work—from mechanical repairs to maintenance—was done through his own skills, effort, and dedication.

Despite starting with limited resources, the CEO’s hard work, perseverance, and commitment to quality service paid off. Through consistent customer satisfaction and word-of-mouth recommendations, the business steadily grew. What once started as a one-man operation eventually expanded into a full-scale auto care shop.

Today, Papz Baliciado Auto Care Center has a large workshop, multiple skilled staff, and is regularly visited and recommended by many satisfied clients. The shop continues to uphold its mission of delivering reliable, honest, and high-quality automotive service to every customer who walks through its doors.
